# Tax Profiling & Investigation Demo  
### Unified Data. Behavioural Risk. Operational Intelligence.

## Overview

This demo shows how tax and compliance teams can investigate taxpayer risk using a unified, multi-source intelligence environment.

Instead of working across disconnected systems, investigators analyze behaviour across declared tax records, financial transactions, third-party reporting, assets, digital activity, and network relationships — all in one place.

The result: faster, smarter investigations based on evidence, not assumptions.

---

## The Data Foundation

The demo integrates six key data domains:

- **Declared Tax Records** – Income, filing status, outstanding balances  
- **Transaction Intelligence** – Real money flow: deposits, transfers, payments  
- **Third-Party Reporting** – Independent corroboration from external sources  
- **Intelligence Alerts** – Pre-flagged behavioural risk indicators  
- **Asset & Lifestyle Data** – Vehicles and other ownership signals  
- **Digital Economy Data** – Online sellers, orders, payment instruments  

This creates a complete behavioural profile for each taxpayer.

---

## Risk & Anomaly Scoring

### Risk Score  
A composite behavioural score combining transactions, alerts, assets, third-party reporting, and digital exposure.

It answers: **Which cases deserve attention first?**

### Anomaly Score  
Measures the gap between declared income and observed financial activity.

The larger the discrepancy, the higher the potential under-reporting risk.

---

## How the Demo Works

### Start with One Taxpayer  
Investigators begin with a single ID and immediately see:

- Risk score  
- Anomaly score  
- Declared vs observed totals  
- Asset indicators  
- Digital activity exposure  
- Shared financial instruments  

This creates a single “risk cockpit” view.

---

### Drill into Evidence  
Each signal links directly to:

- Income declarations  
- Transaction history  
- Third-party records  
- Assets  
- eCommerce sellers, orders, and payments  

Every insight is traceable to evidence.

---

### Discover Hidden Networks  
Using graph analysis, investigators can:

- Detect shared bank accounts  
- Identify coordinated payment behaviour  
- Reveal linked taxpayers  
- Trace exposure to risky sellers  

This turns isolated cases into connected intelligence.

---

### AI-Assisted Investigation  
An embedded AI assistant helps by:

- Summarizing risk signals  
- Highlighting discrepancies  
- Suggesting document requests  
- Recommending next investigation steps  

This accelerates case preparation and audit planning.
