# Siren Jira Case Management Add-on

## Overview
The **Siren Jira Case Management Add-on** integrates Jira with the Siren platform to provide a unified case and investigation management environment. It synchronizes Jira issues, metadata, and relationships into Siren, enabling investigators and analysts to manage cases, track workflows, and analyze operational activity alongside intelligence data.

This add-on bridges operational case tracking with Sirenâ€™s analytics, search, and graph capabilities, allowing teams to move seamlessly between investigation data and case management workflows.

---

## What It Does

The Siren Jira Case Management Add-on:

- Synchronizes Jira issues and linked cases into Siren  
- Indexes case data into Elasticsearch for search and analysis  
- Visualizes case relationships using Siren graph analytics  
- Enables investigators to view and manage case activity within investigation context  
- Connects operational workflows with intelligence datasets  
- Supports dashboards, reporting, and case monitoring  

Jira issues become structured entities inside Siren, allowing analysts to explore relationships between cases, entities, and events.

## How It Works

1. Jira issues are retrieved via API  
2. Data is processed and normalized through ingestion pipelines  
3. Case data and relationships are indexed into Elasticsearch  
4. Siren visualizes cases, links, and entities  
5. Investigators analyze and manage cases within Siren  

This creates a unified operational and investigative view across systems.
