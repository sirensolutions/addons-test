# Siren Law Enforcement  
### Unified Intelligence for Modern Policing

## Overview

Siren Law Enforcement provides investigators with a connected intelligence platform that unifies agency data, forensic evidence, and external intelligence into one operational environment.

Instead of searching across disconnected systems, officers and analysts can investigate people, vehicles, devices, locations, and events within a single workspace.

---

## Multi-Source Intelligence Integration

Siren connects to:

- Agency databases and case systems  
- Digital forensic tools (mobile, computer, cloud extractions)  
- License plate and vehicle records  
- Communication and messaging data  
- Financial and transaction data  
- External intelligence vendors (OSINT, dark web, corporate records, crypto, and more)

All information becomes searchable, linkable, and ready for analysis.
